THE 4 RULES OF GROWTH

1

Add a little every week

Beat last week — one more rep, or +5 lb once you hit the top of the rep range cleanly. Log your weights so you know.

2

Train near failure

The last 1–2 reps of each set should be genuinely hard. If you could've done 5 more, go heavier.

3

Control the negative

Lower the weight slowly (2–3 sec). The lowering phase builds as much muscle as the lift.

4

Eat & sleep to build

Slight calorie surplus, ~0.7–1g protein per lb of bodyweight, 7–9 hrs sleep. Muscle is built in recovery.
